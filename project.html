PakPiston

PakPiston is a dynamic and informative website that caters to the automotive enthusiast and professional mechanic alike. Built on the WordPress platform, PakPiston serves as a comprehensive online resource dedicated to the world of vehicle pistons and rings. This project has been created with a passion for engines and a commitment to providing a one-stop destination for valuable insights, knowledge, and products related to these crucial engine components.

The website is designed with user experience in mind. It boasts an intuitive and easy-to-navigate layout, ensuring that visitors can quickly find the information they seek or browse through the product offerings with ease.

Made With: WordPress

Status: Active

Link: https://pakpiston.com/

Burganic

Burganic is a visually enticing and delectable website created for a unique dining experience, where the world of fast food collides with the art of sushi. Crafted using HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap, this website not only serves as a digital storefront but also reflects the restaurant's fusion of two distinct culinary worlds.

The website offers an interactive menu that allows customers to explore the diverse range of offerings.

Built with Bootstrap, the website is fully responsive, ensuring that customers can access the menu and place orders from any device, whether it's a smartphone, tablet, or desktop computer.

Made With: HTML, CSS, BootStrap

Status: Draft
